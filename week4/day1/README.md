LLMs - Large language models 
AI system trained on massive amounts of text datato understand and generate human like language 
Core building blocks:
1) TOKENS 
Text are broken into Tokens (words,subwords,characters)
eg- My name is Aayushi 
["My","Name","is","Aayushi]

2) Embeddings
Token are converted into numerical vectors 
It captures meaning and relationships between the words 

3) Attention mechanism 
Allows the model to only focus on relvant parts of the data from the sentence

4) Pretraining the model
Teaching an AI model by showing it huge amounts of text and making it repeatedly guess the next word.



Zero shot prompting 
Asking the AI to do a task directly without giving examples.

eg- Translate hello in german

Few shot prompting 
Asking the AI to do task but here by giving examples 

Hello in japanese is konnichiwa
